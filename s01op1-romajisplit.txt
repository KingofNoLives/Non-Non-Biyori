Spaced for karaoke

魚になって空は海
sakanani natte sorawa umi
Turning into a fish, the sky

飛んでるように泳いでる
tonnderu youni oyoideru
swims through the sea as if flying

あの山を一つ越えたら見えてくる
ano yamao hitotsu koetara mietekuru
Once we pass that mountain we'll be able to see

まっすぐに伸びたあぜ道に
massuguni nobita azemichini
On the dirt path stretching straight ahead

誰かのちっちゃな忘れ物
darekano chicchana wasuremono
Something small left, forgotten

かたっぽになった靴が示すは晴れ
katapponi natta kutsuga shimesuwa hare
A missing shoe betrays the warm sunshine [Note: I'm using "betray" here in the sense of revealing, because I'm having trouble coming up with something that sounds ok]

ゆるやかに続いてく
yu ru ya ka ni tsudzu i te ku
Floating slowly along,

日々は答えなど無い
hibiwa kotaenado nai
The days don't give any answers

季節が水を染めて
kisetsuga mizuo somete
The water, colored by the seasons

七色に光るよ
nanaironi hikaruyo
Shines like a rainbow

息継ぎしたら消えた
ikitsugi shitara kieta
With just a breath it went away

絶え間なく降り注いでる
taemanaku furisosoideru
The far-off laughter

笑い声が遠くで
waraigoega tookude
doesn't stop raining down,

歌うように響いてる
utau youni hibiiteru
echoing like a song
